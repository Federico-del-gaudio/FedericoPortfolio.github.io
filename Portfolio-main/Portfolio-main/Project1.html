PUMMEL 2

2.5D Action platformer

About the Project

One of the projects I designed on my own while attending high school, which encompasses everything I learned independently. The game is designed with a simple approach: the player's objective is to search for unstable fragments across 5 distinct worlds. The level sequence is designed to immerse the player in the action right away. From the very first level, the player is teleported directly into the arena, where they will have a dialogue with the future boss. This intentional choice was made to ensure an engaging experience from the very beginning of the game.
