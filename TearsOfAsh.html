About the Project

First game developed with a team of four people for a horror-themed game jam. Step into a chilling horror experience inspired by P.T., where memories, dreams, reality, and guilt blend into a twisted nightmare. Navigate through a haunting atmosphere as you confront disturbing visions and unravel the secrets buried in your mind. Each fragment of your past holds a clue, challenging you to connect the dots and uncover the dark truth lurking beneath the surface.
